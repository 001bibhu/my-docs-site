# Dashboard Overview

The dashboard gives you a complete view of how users interact with your product.

## Key sections

### Search Insights

Understand what users are searching for and where they are struggling.

### Top Issues

View the most common problems users are facing.

### Ticket Trends

Track how support tickets are increasing or decreasing over time.

### Recommendations

AI-generated suggestions to improve product experience and reduce friction.

---

Use the dashboard regularly to monitor trends and identify areas of improvement.
