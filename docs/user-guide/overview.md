# Overview

## What is ProductHelp AI?

ProductHelp AI is an AI-powered insights platform that helps teams understand user behavior, identify product pain points, and reduce support tickets.

It combines intelligent search, usage insights, and contextual recommendations to help teams take faster and more informed decisions.

---

## How does it benefit you?

- Reduces customer support tickets by helping users find answers faster
- Surfaces key product issues from user behavior and search patterns
- Helps product and support teams understand what users struggle with
- Improves self-service experience for customers

---

## Who should use this?

- Product Managers → to identify feature gaps and user pain points
- Support Teams → to reduce incoming tickets and improve resolution time
- Customer Success Teams → to proactively guide users
- End Users → to quickly find answers without raising tickets
